Pay with your mobile phone
Wondr App★ BEST PRICE 100 kr
  1. 1 Register (one time only) at bjerredssaltsjobad.wondr.se
  2. 2 Download the Wondr app from the App Store or Google Play
  3. 3 Open the app and select Single Entry – the gate opens

Next visit you only need the app – no need to register again.

SMS 120 kr
  1. 1 Send an SMS with ccasvo to 72456
  2. 2 You will receive a code on your phone
  3. 3 Enter the code on the keypad at the gate
Swish / Card 120 kr
  1. 1 Go to cckod.se on your phone
  2. 2 Enter terminal ID: ASVO
  3. 3 Pay and enter the code you receive on the keypad at the gate
Coincode App 120 kr
  1. 1 Search for Coincode in the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android)
  2. 2 Pay in the app and enter the code at the gate

More information: www.coincode.se

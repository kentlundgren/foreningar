To the General Assembly of Kalmar Nation at Lund University

Mandate and framework

As a lay auditor elected by the General Assembly, I have reviewed the accounts of Kalmar Nation at Lund University for the financial year 2025 (1 January–31 December 2025). The review has been conducted in accordance with the association's statutes (§ 83), the Swedish Bookkeeping Act (1999:1078), and accepted auditing practice for lay auditors in non-profit associations.

Accounts review

The review has included, among other things:

  • Bank reconciliation as of 31 December 2025: All six SEB accounts, petty cash (SEK 19) and PayPal (SEK 628) match the bank's engagement summary exactly.
  • Accuracy of the balance sheet: The balance sheet balances. Assets = liabilities + equity.
  • Link between result and equity: The corrected surplus of SEK 434,975 matches the change in equity, following reversal of voucher Y2 (see finding 1).
  • Continuity: Closing balance 2024 = Opening balance 2025, and Closing balance 2025 = Opening balance 2026 – confirmed item by item.
  • Sample – supplier invoices: Three invoices reviewed against the voucher list and supporting documentation: A1 (Siemens Financial Services, SEK 8,026), A22 (Martin & Servera, SEK 119,693) and A452 (Lundgrens i Gräfsnäs, SEK 103). No material findings.
  • Inventories: Inventory value was corrected from SEK 423,708 to SEK 298,714 following a physical count. The correction is well-founded. A remaining difference of SEK 12,563 on account 1407 (deposit inventory) has been explained and assessed as a timing matter.

Governance review

I have reviewed the activity report (verksamhetsberättelse) for 2025. The association's activities are assessed to have been conducted in accordance with the statutes and the purpose clause in § 1. No matters have been identified that give cause for remark regarding the Board's governance.

Findings

1. Erroneous circular accounting entry – voucher Y2 (SEK 881,657) During the review it was identified that voucher Y2 (debit account 2099 "Current year result" / credit account 8999, SEK 881,657) is an incorrect entry. The Prokurator ekonomi manually recorded the year's result as a new revenue item in the belief that this was required for the result to be captured in the accounts. The entry is circular – the result included itself – and inflated the calculated result by SEK 881,657.
Action: The Prokurator ekonomi has confirmed the error. Reversal of voucher Y2 has been requested and must be completed before the accounts are adopted. The correct surplus after reversal amounts to SEK 434,975.

Result for the year

The surplus for the year amounts to SEK 434,975 (organic operating result SEK 419,046 plus inventory adjustments SEK 15,929). The amount includes a net financial result of SEK 383,743 comprising returns on managed funds and investments as well as scholarship disbursements. Equity as of 31 December 2025 amounts to SEK 9,942,668.

The Board proposes that the surplus of SEK 434,975 be transferred in full to retained surplus.

Opinion

Based on my review, I recommend:

  • that the income statement and balance sheet for the financial year 2025 be adopted, provided that voucher Y2 has been reversed in accordance with the request,
  • that the surplus of SEK 434,975 be transferred in full to retained surplus,
  • that the members of the Board (seniorskollegiet) be granted discharge of liability towards the association's members for the financial year 2025.

Lund, [date]

Kent Lundgren
Lay auditor, elected by the General Assembly

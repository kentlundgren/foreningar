About the review

The audit of the 2026 annual accounts will take place in early 2027.
Kalmar Nation's financial year runs 1 January – 31 December 2026. The annual accounts are finalised by the PQe and submitted to the auditor after the new year. This page will be updated as the review progresses.

An audit is an independent check of the association's finances – a bit like having an outsider double-check that the cash book adds up. The audit covers two parts: accounts review and governance review.

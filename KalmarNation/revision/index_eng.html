Kalmar Nation – audit 2025

Financial review (lay auditor) of Kalmar Nation's annual accounts for the financial year 2025, Lund. The audit is ongoing; a complete audit report will be prepared once the review is finished.

About the review

A financial review checks that the association's accounts give a fair picture of its finances – that money has been recorded correctly, that bank balances match, and that there are no unreasonable discrepancies. The auditor reviews, explains and identifies issues that need to be addressed.

Review notes as of 2026-03-26

File: Revisionsanteckningar_KalmarNation_2025_per_260326.pdf (11 pages) · Initial review

Bank reconciliation

All six SEB accounts match exactly with the bank's account summary as of 31 December 2025. In addition, cash (SEK 19) and PayPal (SEK 628) are correctly recorded in the accounts. Approved

Result and equity

Calculated result 2025: +SEK 505,780 (after inventory adjustment). The relationship result = change in equity matches exactly. Without the financial income (dividends etc. from foundation securities, +SEK 579,000), the operating activities would have shown a modest surplus of approximately SEK 48,000. Approved

Discrepancy in inventory (CB 2025 ≠ OB 2026)

A difference of SEK 137,557 was identified in the beverage inventory (beer, cider, wine, spirits, draught beer) between the closing balance 2025 and the opening balance 2026. Likely explanation: a physical stocktake was carried out at or shortly after the year-end and showed lower balances than the figures calculated in the accounts. The question is whether the write-down should be charged to 2025 or 2026 – this depends on when the stocktake actually took place. Explanation required

Comparative analysis 2025 vs 2024

  • Total revenue increased by +37% (+SEK 1.2m).
  • Costs increased by +31% – a lower growth rate than revenue. Positive.
  • Operating result improved from −SEK 117,000 (2024) to +SEK 48,000 (2025).
  • Staff costs decreased by −14% compared to 2024.
  • Consumables more than doubled (+112%) – worth monitoring.
